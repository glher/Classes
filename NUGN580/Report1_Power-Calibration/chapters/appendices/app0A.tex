\chapter{Adjusted heat constant}
\label{app:app01}

\initial{T}his appendix presents the adjusted heat constant for the USGS GSTR as a function of the level of water in the tank. A correction of 0.35\% per inches of water below the upper lip is applied.

        14 inches & 28.495 \\
        15 inches & 28.595 \\ 
        \end{tabular}
